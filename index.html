WHERE

Haut Bois Normand

426 Chemin George Bonnallie, Eastman, Québec J0E 1P0

Haut Bois Normand is a charming old-fashioned sugar shack.

ACCOMODATION

There are a few options for accomodation:

Haut Bois Normand hostel

We are reserving the entire hostel for the night of the wedding. We are thinking this option will be best for the people that plan to stay up late partying. Ariane and John will be doing the arrangements for this, but payment will still be made to Haut Bois Normand (~$30). Please contact Ariane or John directly if you are interested.

Le Vertendre Eco Chalets

These are beautiful eco-chalets in the woods and are within a mile of the venue. They can fit between 4 and 14 people. Note that they require a 2-night stay and not all of them have drive-up access. Contact them directly ASAP if you are interested as we expect these to be quite popular in the summer. When reserving, mention that you are visiting for our wedding. If enough people book then a discount may be given.

Motel Du Village

A basic motel 5km away. We are working to block off a portion of rooms in the motel for people to reserve. You are of course welcome to reserve before we do this.
